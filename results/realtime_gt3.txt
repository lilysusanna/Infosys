Indian Foods are famous for their diversity, flavors, and use of spices, reflecting the country’s cultural and regional variety. Every region has its own specialties—North India is known for roti, paratha, and rich curries, South India for idli, dosa, and sambar, East India for rice, fish, and sweets like rasgulla, and West India for dhokla, puran poli, and spicy snacks. Indian cuisine emphasizes balance of taste, nutrition, and aroma, using ingredients like rice, wheat, lentils, vegetables, herbs, and spices.
